Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our guidance and counselling services.

At what age should my child start career counselling?

We recommend starting career exploration as early as middle school (ages 12-14), with more intensive counselling in high school. Early exposure helps students make informed course selections that align with potential career paths. Our age-appropriate assessments and activities make the process engaging for younger students.

How often should we schedule counselling sessions?

Frequency depends on the student's needs and goals. Most students benefit from bi-weekly sessions initially, transitioning to monthly check-ins. During critical periods (like college application season), weekly sessions may be recommended. We'll create a personalized plan after the initial assessment.

Are your counsellors licensed professionals?

Yes, all our mental wellness counsellors are licensed therapists or psychologists. Our career and academic counsellors hold relevant certifications (NCDA, ICF, etc.) and advanced degrees in education or counselling psychology. We maintain the highest professional standards for all our team members.

Do you offer virtual counselling sessions?

Yes, we offer both in-person and virtual sessions via secure video platform. Many students find virtual sessions just as effective and more convenient. All assessment tools and resources are adapted for remote use when needed.

What's your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations to avoid charges. In case of emergencies, we'll work with you to reschedule. Packages of multiple sessions must be used within 12 months of purchase.
