Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course at EduVantage?

Enrolling is simple! Visit our Classes page, select your desired course, and click the "Enroll Now" button. You'll be guided through our secure enrollment process where you can choose your batch timings and complete the payment. If you need assistance, our admissions team is available via chat, email, or phone.

What payment options are available?

We offer multiple payment options for your convenience. You can pay online via credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, or digital wallets. We also support installment plans for most courses. For offline payments, you can visit any of our centers and pay via cash, cheque, or demand draft.

Can I attend demo classes before enrolling?

Absolutely! We encourage prospective students to attend our free demo classes. You can schedule a demo session through our website or by contacting our support team. Demos are available both online and at our physical centers. This gives you a chance to experience our teaching methodology before making a commitment.

What if I miss a class? Are recordings available?

Yes, we understand students may occasionally miss classes. For all our courses, we provide recorded sessions that are accessible through our learning portal for 30 days. Additionally, you can schedule a one-on-one session with your instructor to cover missed content. We recommend attending live classes whenever possible for the best learning experience.
